Help

How to get the most out of Equipment Explorer.

Search

The search page lets you find cables, equipment, and documents in one place. Start typing and autocomplete suggestions will appear after two characters.

Filters:

  • All - Searches cables, equipment, and documents simultaneously.
  • Cables - Limits results to cable tags only.
  • Equipment - Limits results to equipment tags only.
  • Documents - Searches by filename, supplier name, or supplier code.

Tips:

  • Click through connections to quickly build an understanding of how systems are interconnected.
  • When viewing a tag result, view all the documents the tag appears on and filter them as needed.
  • When viewing a document result, view all the tags found within it and filter them as needed.
  • Pin any cable, equipment, or document to the sidebar.

Cable List

A full overview of all cables with their connection endpoints, compartment locations, and deck information. Use the table search to filter by any field.

  • Click any cable or equipment tag to open it in search.
  • Sort by any column by clicking its header.
  • Export the current view to CSV to print out a filtered cable list.
  • Pin a cable to the sidebar for quick access later.

Documents

Browse all drawings and technical documents. Each document shows its indexing status, supplier information, and the number of tags found inside.

Status indicators:

  • Indexed - Document has been processed; tags are searchable.
  • Pending - Document is queued for indexing.
  • Not Indexed - Document is not yet in the index.

Actions:

  • Open - Opens the PDF in a new tab
  • Contents - Shows all cables and equipment found in the document
  • Pin - Pins the document to the sidebar for quick access later
